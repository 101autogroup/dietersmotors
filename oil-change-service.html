Oil Changes for European Vehicles

European vehicles like BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Porsche, Mini and Volvo require specific oil types and change intervals. Using the wrong oil or stretching service too far can lead to sludge buildup, leaks and premature engine wear.

How Often Should You Change Your Oil?

Recommended oil change intervals vary by vehicle, oil type and driving conditions. Many modern vehicles can go 5,000–10,000 miles between oil changes, but frequent short trips or heavy driving may require more frequent service. We can help you set the right schedule for your car or truck.
