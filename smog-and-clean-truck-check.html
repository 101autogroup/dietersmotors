Smog & Clean Truck Check in Oxnard

California’s emissions standards are strict, and vehicles must pass smog testing to stay registered. For diesel trucks, Clean Truck Check requirements add another layer of compliance.

At Dieter’s Motors in Oxnard, we provide smog-related diagnostics and repair as well as support for Clean Truck Check readiness for drivers and businesses in Ventura County.

Smog & Emissions Services

  • Pre-smog inspections
  • Emissions diagnostics and repair
  • Catalytic converter and O2 sensor evaluations
  • EVAP system leak diagnostics
  • Check engine light diagnosis for emissions failures

Clean Truck Check Support

Diesel trucks must stay compliant with California emissions programs. We can help identify and repair issues that may cause a truck to fail testing, including:

  • Diesel particulate filter (DPF) concerns
  • NOx sensor and SCR system faults
  • Exhaust leaks and opacity issues
  • General diesel engine diagnostics

Help for Vehicles that Fail Smog

If your vehicle fails smog, we can perform a diagnostic inspection to find out why, then quote a repair plan to help bring your car back into compliance.
