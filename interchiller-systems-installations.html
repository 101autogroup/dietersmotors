Interchiller Systems Installations in Oxnard

High-performance engines generate a lot of heat, especially in boosted applications with turbochargers or superchargers. Excessive heat reduces power and can stress internal components.

At Dieter’s Motors in Oxnard, we offer Interchiller systems installations for performance vehicles across Ventura County, helping drivers lower intake air temperatures and achieve more consistent power.

What Is an Interchiller System?

An Interchiller system uses the vehicle’s air conditioning circuit to chill the intercooler fluid, significantly reducing intake air temperatures (IAT). Cooler air is denser, allowing more oxygen into the engine and improving performance.

Benefits of an Interchiller Installation

  • Lower intake air temperatures under load
  • Reduced heat soak during repeated pulls or hot weather
  • More consistent horsepower on the street and track
  • Improved reliability for high-boost engines
