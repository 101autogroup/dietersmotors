Automotive Diagnostics in Oxnard

Modern vehicles rely on advanced electronics, modules and sensors. When something goes wrong, you need more than a simple code reader—you need a shop with real diagnostic tools and experience.

At Dieter’s Motors in Oxnard, we specialize in automotive diagnostics for European and domestic vehicles, serving drivers in Oxnard, Camarillo and all of Ventura County.

What We Diagnose

  • Check engine lights and stored fault codes
  • Electrical issues and wiring faults
  • Fuel system and air/fuel ratio problems
  • Turbo and boost control issues
  • ABS, traction control and stability system warnings
  • Hybrid and battery system alerts
  • Transmission error messages
  • Emissions and smog-related problems

European Diagnostic Specialists

We use factory-level diagnostic equipment for BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Porsche, Mini and Volvo. This allows us to see detailed data, run system tests and identify the exact cause of your car’s symptoms.

Why Proper Diagnostics Saves Money

Guessing at parts and replacing components randomly is expensive. Our goal is to find the root cause of the problem and repair it correctly the first time. Accurate diagnostics help you avoid repeat visits and unnecessary parts.
