Brake Repair Service in Oxnard

Your brakes are the most important safety system on your vehicle. Worn brake pads, warped rotors or failing ABS components can put you and your passengers at risk, especially in stop-and-go traffic around Oxnard, Camarillo and Ventura County.

At Dieter’s Motors in Oxnard, we provide complete brake repair and replacement for European and domestic vehicles, including BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Porsche, Mini, Volvo, trucks and SUVs.

Signs You Need Brake Service

  • Squeaking or grinding noises when braking
  • Vibration in the steering wheel or pedal
  • Soft or spongy brake pedal feel
  • Increased stopping distance
  • Brake warning light on your dash

Brake Services We Offer

  • Brake pad and rotor replacement
  • Brake fluid flush and bleeding
  • ABS and traction control diagnostics
  • Caliper inspection, repair and replacement
  • Parking brake adjustments and repair
  • Performance brake upgrades for European vehicles

European & Domestic Brake Repair

We use high-quality components that meet or exceed factory specifications. Whether you drive a European sedan, an SUV or a work truck, we make sure your brakes perform the way they should.

Schedule Brake Repair in Oxnard

If you notice any brake issues or noises, don’t wait. Prompt brake service can prevent further damage and keep you safe.
